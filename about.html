About
Hi there!

I’m Yue, a media and communication student who calls both Hangzhou and Melbourne home. My journey with photography started back in junior high when I got my first camera--a moment that sparked what has now become my greatest passion. Photography isn’t just a hobby for me; it’s a way to invite others into my world, to share the stories and scenes I encounter every day. I'm always with my camera, ready to capture the raw and authentic beauty of the world and its people. Through my lens, I strive to document genuine moments that resonate and connect, highlighting the ordinary yet profound connections we share. Welcome to my portfolio—where each image tells a unique story, and every subject shines in their unguarded truth.


  • For a comprehensive look at my work, my folio document is available for download. It encompasses a wide range of projects, showcasing the versatility and depth of my photographic endeavors.
  • For collaborations, inquiries, or just to say hi, feel free to to contact me or follow me on Social Media. I am always open to discussing new projects or opportunities.
